How to play: Gamemode "What"


Gamemode “What” assumes you’re already familiar with the rules of regular Rock, Paper, Scissors, as well as how to play it on this website.

Five out of six cards have been replaced with a random emoji, your task is to identify whether one of them is a rock, paper, or scissors. The emoji you’ll be identifying is shown in the lower section of the screen.

The duel is played as usual, but keep an eye on the pink announcement board at the top to see who is winning and which emoji is highlighted.

To determine the winner;
“<“ means the computer wins,
“>” means the player wins,
“=“ means it’s a draw.

Play a few rounds until you figure it out.

You can identify the emoji at the bottom of the screen. To make a guess, select either rock, paper, or scissors and click “Submit” at the lower section of the screen.

The pink announcement board at the top will tell you how well you did, and the answer will be revealed in the lower section.

Click "Next Game" to play again.
